Support & FAQs

Find answers to common questions and access support resources. We're here to help you succeed on your learning journey.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register for an account?

To register for an account, click on the "Register" link in the header or on the dashboard page and follow the instructions.

How do I download printable resources?

To download printable resources, go to the "Printable Resources" section and select the grade and subject you need. The resources are free and do not require a login.

How do I track my child's progress?

You can track your child's progress by logging in to your account and accessing the "Dashboard" section.

What subjects are available?

We offer a variety of subjects, including Mathematics, Language Arts, Science, Social Studies, and practical skills like Subsistence Farming, Plumbing, and Basic Electricity.
